GTA 6 Facts

GTA 6 is set in Vice City, Rockstar’s version of Miami.
It features the series’ first female protagonist, Lucia.
The game is rumored to release in May 2026.
The open world is said to be the largest Rockstar has ever made.
The game will include improved AI and more realistic NPC behavior.
You can switch between characters seamlessly like in GTA V.
Rockstar confirmed new physics and vehicle destruction systems.
Weather and time systems will dynamically affect gameplay.
