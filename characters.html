GTA 6 Characters

Lucia

Life’s been coming at her swinging ever since. Fighting for her family landed her in Leonida Penitentiary — sheer luck got her out. Now, she’s all smart moves from here.

Jason

Grew up around criminals and served in the military before finding his way into the Keys’ drug trade. Sharp, quiet, and lethal when he needs to be.

Dre'Quan Preist

A hustler turned artist — from the streets to the studio, Dre’Quan’s chasing the high of success the legal way now, one track at a time.

Stefanie

A social worker for the Leonida Department of Corrections, she’s seen the system’s cracks — and plans to fix them her own way.

Cal Hampton

Jason’s friend and fellow associate of Brian’s. Prefers his home lab, beers, and Coast Guard comms — but don’t underestimate his intel.

Boobie Ike

A Vice City legend. Street life funded his empire — real estate, clubs, studios. He smiles big, but business stays deadly serious.

Real Dimez

Bae-Luxe and Roxy — aka Real Dimez — flipped street hustle into social stardom. Music, money, and Miami heat — they run it all.

Raul Bautista

Confidence and charm define Raul, a veteran bank robber with a keen eye for talent and a thirst for high-stakes thrills.

Brian Heder

Old-school drug runner with decades in the Keys. He lets younger crews handle the dirty work now — but his empire’s still afloat.

Luxe

Bae-Luxe here — Vice City’s finest. From dealer shake-downs to viral fame, she and Roxy built an empire off grit and glow.
